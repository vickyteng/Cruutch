Gain Points to Donate to Charity

Gain points by posting reviews about how disability-friendly businesses are. Details such as door widths, spacing between tables, and bathroom accessibility can be crucial drivers in deciding what places individuals with disabilities may consider visiting. We would like to make society more disability-friendly and have more accessible spaces.

You are rewarded one point per review you write, and you can build up points to donate money to charity! You raise $1 for every 10 points or reviews you write, which you can contribute to a disabilities non-profit or charity of your choice!
